01. Mission Statement

FEGSOL is engineered to bridge the gap between community-driven meme culture and sustainable decentralized finance. Our goal is to create a hyper-deflationary ecosystem on Solana where long-term holders are rewarded through systematic supply reduction.

02. The Burn Protocol

Unlike traditional memecoins, $FEGSOL utilizes a Monthly Buyback & Burn strategy to ensure a constantly diminishing supply and increased scarcity.

  • Monthly Buyback: A portion of the marketing fund will be used to buy back $FEGSOL from the open market.
  • Permanent Burn: Tokens acquired via buyback will be sent to the Solana Null Address (Burn), removing them from circulation forever.
  • Public Proof: All burn transactions (TXIDs) will be published on our official channels for full transparency.
